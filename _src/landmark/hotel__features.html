Hotel Features

The Landmark Hotel offers you great service and hospitality. Our Basic and Deluxe Rooms feature full menu room service, free local telephone calls , in-room coffee makers, plus a fridge in every room.

Wake up the right way; with a free cup of coffee and a complimentary copy of the local morning Chronicle Journal newspaper!

Need to do a little business on the road, take advantage of our free High-Speed Internet and wireless service.

Use our complimentary Internet kiosk located in the lobby to check your mail, make on-line reservations, or search for information.

For those travelers who require a little extra during their stay, our Deluxe Room is the perfect choice. These spacious accommodations offer a queen-sized bed, and a upgraded work area suitable for small meetings.

While in Thunder Bay, check out some the the following local attractions:

Fort William Historical Park - takes you on an incredible journey back in time!
Amethyst Mines - let you discover for yourself!
Sleeping Giant Provincial Park - Offers an incredible view! Kakabeka Falls Provincial Park - See the spectacular falls, beach, and picnic facilities!

Magnus Theatre - Local Theatre at its best.
Ouimet Canyon Provincial Park - Offers an Incredible view or Eagle Canyon to walk the thrilling suspension bridges!

Middle Falls Provincial Park - Offers camping, canoeing, or hiking along the Pigeon River!
Terry Fox Monument and Visitor Centre - An Incredible view, information and a tribute to a formidable man.
Thunder Bay Art Gallery - for inspiration!

Thunder Bay Events and Festivals - Sibley Tour, Thunder Bay Blues Festival, Pow-Wow's, Thunder Bay Fringe Festival, Pumpkin Fest, Christmas Fantasy just to name a few!

Founders Museum & Pioneer Village - An immense display of pioneer history and artifacts!
Snowmobilers Take Note! - Thunder Bay is your "gateway to great snowmobiling."
